What is Insurrection?

Insurrection is a tower defense video game developed. The game involves 7 units and a buff system. The goal of the game is to go through all levels coming up with different strategies for defeating the enemy.

Technologies

Gameplay

To start the game, the player selects any available unit in the current round, after which the enemies begin to attack. In each round, different units are available. The unit's stability is indicated under their icons. So the currency is earned by killing the enemy and passing the round. The player can temporarily increase the damage or life units on the battlefield.
